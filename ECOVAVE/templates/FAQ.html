Do I need a license to ride an ECOVAVE e-bike?

In India, our e-bikes are classified as electric bicycles and don't require a license or registration as they comply with government policies:

  • Motor power under 250W
  • Speed limited to 25 km/h
  • Compliant with EV regulations

Note: Some high-performance models may require registration - please check your chosen model's specifications.

Maintenance Questions

How often does an e-bike need servicing?

We recommend the following maintenance schedule:

First Service

After 500 km or 1 month (whichever comes first)

Regular Service

Every 3,000 km or 6 months

Battery Check

Every 6 months

E-bikes generally require less maintenance than petrol vehicles with fewer moving parts and no engine oil changes.

Can I ride my e-bike in the rain?

Yes, you can ride in rain

ECOVAVE e-bikes are water-resistant

But avoid

Deep puddles or submerged areas

Rain Riding Tips:

  • Dry the bike after heavy rain
  • Don't charge when wet
  • Store in dry place
