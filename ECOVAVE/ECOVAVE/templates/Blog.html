Essential Winter Care for Your E-Bike

March 10, 2025 5 min read Share

Winter conditions can significantly impact your e-bike's performance and longevity. Follow these expert recommendations to keep your ECOVAVE running optimally through cold months:

Battery Care

  • Store batteries indoors when not in use
  • Charge at room temperature (15-25°C)
  • Keep charge between 30-70% for storage

Cleaning & Protection

  • Wipe down after wet/salty rides
  • Apply silicone spray to electrical contacts
  • Check tire pressure weekly
